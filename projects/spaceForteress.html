Gameplay Overview

In Space Fortress, players must collect in-game currency as they defend the ship, which they can then spend on valuable upgrades and reinforcements. This currency allows players to buy new turrets, traps, and powerful weapons to help fend off the growing waves of invaders. Each round brings new types of enemies, requiring players to strategize effectively and use their resources wisely.

Key Features

  • Strategic Resource Management: Players balance their earnings to upgrade firepower, add new defenses, and place traps around critical reactor areas.
  • Rogue-like Elements: Each playthrough is unique, with random wave patterns and enemy types that challenge players to adapt their strategies.
  • Upgrade System: Unlock advanced weapons, turrets, and defenses as the game progresses, helping players hold off increasingly tough enemies.

Space Fortress Combines the high-stakes action of a rogue-like game with the deep, tactical planning of a strategy game. Each wave is a test of skill, making every decision crucial for survival as players defend the reactor against the oncoming onslaught. Prepare to think quickly, act strategically, and test your defensive skills in Space Fortress!
